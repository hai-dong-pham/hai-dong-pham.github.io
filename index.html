    My name is Hai Pham - ex-financial analyst, ex-investment banker, now coder. With six years of experience in financial and data analysis and education in dual degree MBA/MSBA program, I am skillful at employing financial modeling on forecasting and planning, leveraging visualization tools on delivering presentations, and applying data analytics tools, such as SQL, Tableau, Python, Excel to solving business problems.

I am interested in combining my diverse financial expertise with data analytics to come up with actionable insights and data-driven decisions.
